OLA Shakti BESS — Complete E/E Architecture
OLA Electric · 2024–Present

Designed the full electrical & electronics architecture for a 5–10kWh grid-tied Battery Energy Storage System. System integrates an 8KVA inverter, dual 3.3KW AC/DC chargers in parallel, DC-DC converter, Hall & CT current sensors, relay protection matrix, solar input, and ITCU control unit with CAN/BLE/LTE communication.
